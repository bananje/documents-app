Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

This extension requires access to your Google Drive to allow you to quickly use and manage your documents directly from your browser. We take your privacy seriously and explain below how your data is handled.

Information We Access

  • The extension only accesses files and folders you explicitly select in Google Drive.
  • We do not read, copy, or share your data beyond the intended functionality of the extension.
  • No personal identifiers (name, email, browsing history) are collected outside of the Google Drive API.

How We Use Your Data

  • The accessed files are used only to provide the features of the extension.
  • No data is stored or transmitted to external servers. All processing happens locally in your browser or via Google Drive API.

Permissions

  • The extension requests Google Drive API permissions to open, read, and modify your files.
  • These permissions are strictly limited to the features described above.

Third-Party Services

  • We do not use third-party services to track or collect user data.

Security

  • Your data is handled according to Google's security standards.
  • We do not share your Google Drive credentials or files with anyone.
